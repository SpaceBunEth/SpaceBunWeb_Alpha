SpaceBun

Spacebun is a brand, character, and artist. As a brand Spacebun hopes to create a creative environment for individuals looking to express themselves. Developing and providing the necessary tools to spark creativity and community for artist of all kinds. The character spacebun acts a mascot and face to brand, providing a sense of youth and innocence. As well as a medium for story telling and perspective of the artist. The artist spacebun is an individual set on creating something for all ages and looks to share ideas and methods for making the world a better place.
